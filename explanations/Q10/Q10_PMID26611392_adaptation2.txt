Nerve-related, upper hand pain greatly affects patient performance and quality of life, even with proper treatment.
This article looks at leading treatments that may improve pain care, including advanced treatments that are available.
This article also reviews upcoming therapies, like spinal cord electrical stimulation, peripheral or outer nerve stimulation, and dorsal root ganglion spinal cord stimulation (electrical treatment for specific cells in the spinal cord).
As these therapies improve, so will their use in pain care grounded by evidence to improve patient safety and monitoring of patients with complex nerve-related pain.
