What causes muscle spasm?

Muscle cramps are common in healthy people, especially in the elderly and young people after intense exercise.
Cramps are in many harmless brain-related disorders.
Cramps are common in long-lasting, brain-related disorders like amyotrophic lateral sclerosis which weakens muscles.
We reviewed the basis and biological mechanisms of cramps.
Future studies are needed to understand cramps.
Current treatment is limited.
