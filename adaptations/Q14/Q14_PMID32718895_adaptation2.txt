How to increase body immunity against covid 19?

The immune system protects against viruses and diseases and makes specific proteins to kill specific disease-causing invaders.
This review discusses the protection of the immune system against COVID-19 (a viral respiratory disease); how the immune system works and fights diseases; and the most recent COVID-19 treatment.
Various challenges for the immune system are also discussed.
At the article's end, phyical exercise and certain dietary suggestions are encouraged.
